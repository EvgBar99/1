Membership

Becoming our club member you have the opportunity to use all the zones of the Club regardless your membership package: Gym, Group Workouts, Water Zone (Pool, Rooftop pool & Jacuzzi in summer season, Jacuzzi, Hamam and Finnish Sauna). The usage of the gym by kids under 14 is allowed only with a personal coach.
