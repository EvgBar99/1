About Us

Being founded in 2008 we had the goal of developing fitness culture in Armenia. Today we are more than fitness, therefore, our mission is to develop club approach in fitness. Our club members are successful people preferring sport, healthy and beautiful lifestyle, as well as active leisure. Each of them finds in the Club what they need.





Orange Fitness Premium Club functions within Kinetik CJSC, including also Incourt Tennis Club.  


Our values

Friendly

We are friendly with our club members, partners and employees. Our communication style is respectful, still relaxed and not so official, so that people feel free when communicating to us, as they would feel with their friends.


Trustworthy

We are trustworthy as a club, as a friend and as a partner. People trust us their health, look, leisure, comfort and well being. We do our best to justify their trust.


Caring

We believe good service is very important, but not enough for our club members, as they deserve more. We are caring, which is much more than a good service.


Open-minded

Stereotypes have no place in our daily work, actions and thoughts. We are a club of open-minded people, where every person has a unique value.


Joyful

We are young as far as we are joyful. This is a club of joyful, therefore young people.
