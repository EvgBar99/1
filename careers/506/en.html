Fitness trainer

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Demonstrating exercises and routines to clients
  • Assisting clients in exercises to minimize injury and promote fitness
  • Modify exercises according to clients’ fitness levels
  • Monitoring client progress and adapt programs as needed
  • Explain and enforce safety rules and regulations on sports, recreational activities, and the use of exercise equipment
  • Providing information or resources on general fitness, weight control and health issues
  • Work with people of all ages and skill levels
  • Providing emergency first aid if necessary

 

 

 

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:
- At least 3 years of experience in the relevant field;
- Excellent communication and presentation skills;
- Fluency in Armenian and English languages;
