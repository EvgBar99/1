Why Detox Smoothie?
26 Sep, 2018
Nowadays we often catch ads claiming that we should obtain this or that detox smoothie if we want to shape our body.

But what is detox smoothie?

We live in a world, were we continuously, meet, get acquainted and start using new terms, names and categories.

 

Nowadays we often catch ads claiming that we should obtain this or that detox smoothie if we want to shape our body.

 

But what is detox smoothie? Is it something that anybody is aware of? I am sure, no! The term itself consists of two important categories: detox (detoxication) and smoothie.
Let's put the things together. Detoxication or detox is the elimination of toxic substances from the body. Ah, don't think you don't have toxic substances, we all have them :)

 

And, smoothie is a thick blended beverage with shake like consistency, normally pureed in a blender containing fruits and/or vegetables as well as an added ice or liquid such as water, fruit juice, vegetable juice, milk, or even yogurt.

 

So, detox smoothies are a very delicious way  to detox your body. To combine usefulness with tastiness we have collaborated with professional nutritionist and fitness trainers to create 10 different MustHaveDetoxes at Orange Kitchen. Each of them is developed to assist you in reaching specific goals.

 

Enjoy it, detox your body and have the body you deserve!
